Real-Time Age Estimation System Based on Convolutionla Neural Network

The demand for automatic face detection and age recognition has become increasingly significant for the social media industry and the whole society. Most of the previous age estimation models are based on the presumption that the input images for their models are facial images of human. In the real-world scenario, however, the input might not be qualified enough. Moreover, their estimation performance is yet to be improved. Therefor we worked on improving the age estimation technology.
